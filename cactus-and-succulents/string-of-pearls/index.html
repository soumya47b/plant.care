String of Pearls

Introduction

String of Pearls (Senecio rowleyanus) is a unique succulent with long, trailing stems adorned with spherical, pea-shaped leaves.

It’s a popular choice for hanging baskets and as a decorative indoor plant due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance needs.

How to Grow

  1. Plant in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.
  2. Water sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.
  3. Fertilize with a diluted, balanced fertilizer once during the growing season.
  4. Provide support or a hanging basket to accommodate its trailing growth.
  5. Protect from frost and avoid overhead watering to prevent leaf rot.
