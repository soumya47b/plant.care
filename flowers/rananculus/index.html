Rananculus

Introduction

Ranunculus, commonly known as buttercups, are admired for their bright, cup-shaped flowers that come in various colors.

They add a splash of beauty to gardens and floral arrangements, thriving in cool climates and providing vibrant blooms from spring to early summer.

How to Grow

  1. Plant bulbs in well-draining soil with full to partial sun exposure.
  2. Ensure the soil is kept moist but not waterlogged.
  3. Apply a balanced fertilizer every 4-6 weeks during the growing season.
  4. Protect from extreme heat and dry conditions.
  5. In colder climates, mulch or lift bulbs before frost to overwinter.
