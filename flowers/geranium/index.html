Geranium

Introduction

Geraniums are popular flowering plants known for their vibrant blooms and aromatic foliage. They are easy to grow and thrive in a wide range of climates, making them a favorite in gardens worldwide.

These plants are often grown in containers, garden beds, and hanging baskets, offering color from spring to fall.

How to Grow

  1. Plant in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.
  2. Water regularly but allow the soil to dry out between waterings.
  3. Fertilize every 4-6 weeks during the growing season.
  4. Deadhead spent blooms to encourage new growth.
  5. Bring indoors before frost if grown in colder regions.
