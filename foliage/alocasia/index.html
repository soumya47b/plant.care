Alocasia

Introduction

Alocasia, often known as Elephant Ear, is recognized for its large, dramatic leaves that can vary in shape and color.

They are tropical plants that thrive in warm, humid environments and are popular for their striking foliage in indoor and outdoor settings.

How to Grow

  1. Plant in well-draining, rich soil with bright, indirect light.
  2. Keep the soil consistently moist, but allow the top layer to dry out between waterings.
  3. Fertilize with a balanced, water-soluble fertilizer every 4-6 weeks during the growing season.
  4. Maintain high humidity and protect from drafts and extreme temperatures.
  5. In cooler climates, bring plants indoors or provide winter protection.
