Monstera

Introduction

Monstera, commonly known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, is famous for its large, glossy leaves with unique splits and holes.

It’s a popular indoor plant that adds a tropical touch to any space and thrives in low to bright, indirect light.

How to Grow

  1. Plant in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.
  2. Water when the top inch of soil feels dry; avoid waterlogging.
  3. Fertilize with a balanced houseplant fertilizer every 4-6 weeks during the growing season.
  4. Provide support for the climbing growth habit if needed.
  5. Maintain humidity to encourage optimal leaf development.
